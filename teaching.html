Teaching

My approach to teaching is shaped by my experience designing cybersecurity training exercises for non-specialist audiences, which taught me how to make technical material accessible without losing rigor. In the classroom, I emphasize discussion-based learning, transparent and fair assessment, and transferable skills like critical thinking, problem-solving, and clear writing. I'm comfortable teaching in-person, online, and asynchronous formats, and regularly revise my courses based on student feedback and learning analytics.
