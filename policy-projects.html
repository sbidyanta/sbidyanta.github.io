Alongside my academic research, I work on applied projects that translate cyber strategy concepts into training and exercise tools for practitioners.

PE: The Game

Ohio Cyber Range Initiative (OCRI) — Ohio Persistent Cyber Improvement (OPCI)

PE: The Game is a cyber digital exercise conducted through the Ohio Persistent Cyber Improvement (OPCI) program of the Ohio Cyber Range Initiative (OCRI). The exercise is meant to supplement cybersecurity training for local government entities in Ohio. It has been designed with the principles of cyber persistence theory and the importance of persistent engagement in mind.

My role: I co-authored the moderator and player guides, designed several elements of the game logic, and converted real-world incidents into playable scenarios.

Team: PI — Prof. Richard Harknett; Co-PI — Prof. Michael Loadenthal.

The training program is not yet public. Further details and materials will be added here once available.
